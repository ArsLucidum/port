E-commerce Platform Redesign

Transforming the shopping experience through user-centered design and data-driven insights

Role Lead UX Designer
Duration 6 months
Team 4 designers, 6 developers
Year 2024
UX Research UI Design Prototyping User Testing E-commerce

Project Overview

This project involved a complete redesign of an e-commerce platform serving over 100,000 monthly users. The existing platform suffered from poor conversion rates, confusing navigation, and accessibility issues. Our goal was to create an intuitive, accessible, and conversion-focused shopping experience.

The Challenge

The original platform had a conversion rate of only 1.2%, with user research revealing significant pain points in the checkout process, product discovery, and overall navigation. Mobile users, representing 65% of traffic, were particularly affected by poor responsive design and slow loading times.

Design Process

1

Research & Discovery

Conducted user interviews, surveys, and analytics analysis to understand pain points and user behavior.

2

Information Architecture

Redesigned the site structure and navigation based on card sorting and user journey mapping.

3

Wireframing & Prototyping

Created low and high-fidelity wireframes, followed by interactive prototypes for user testing.

4

Visual Design

Developed a cohesive visual language that aligned with brand guidelines and accessibility standards.

5

Testing & Iteration

Conducted usability testing sessions and iteratively refined the design based on user feedback.

6

Implementation

Collaborated closely with developers to ensure pixel-perfect implementation and smooth launch.

Key Solutions

Design Decisions

Simplified Navigation: Reduced the main navigation from 12 categories to 6, implementing a mega-menu structure that improved discoverability without overwhelming users.

Enhanced Product Pages: Redesigned product pages with better image galleries, clearer pricing information, and social proof elements that increased trust and engagement.

Streamlined Checkout: Implemented a single-page checkout process with guest checkout option, reducing cart abandonment by addressing the most common user friction points.

Results & Impact

The redesigned platform launched successfully and exceeded all initial performance targets, demonstrating the value of user-centered design and thorough research.

+180% Conversion Rate Increase
-45% Cart Abandonment Reduction
+65% Mobile Engagement
4.8/5 User Satisfaction Score
